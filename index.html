🧾 帳號刪除申請流程

如果您希望刪除您的 Risen App 帳號及相關資料，請依以下步驟操作：

  1. 聯絡信箱：請寄信至 support@risenholdings.com.tw
  2. 提供資訊：請附上註冊手機號碼與身分確認資訊。
  3. 處理時間：我們將於 7 個工作天內 刪除帳號與相關個資。

📂 被刪除的資料包含：

  • 帳號登入資訊
  • 租約與通報紀錄
  • 聯絡與系統操作紀錄

⚖️ 法規留存資料

依法需保存之交易或金流資料（如租金帳務記錄），將依相關法規留存 180 天 後再自動銷毀。

如對帳號刪除流程有疑問，請隨時透過 客服信箱 聯絡我們。

🧾 Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your Risen App account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Email: Send a request to support@risenholdings.com.tw
  2. Provide Information: Include your registered phone number and identity details for verification.
  3. Processing Time: We will delete your account and associated data within 7 business days.

📂 Data to Be Deleted:

  • Account login information
  • Rental and report records
  • Communication and system activity logs

⚖️ Data Retention for Legal Compliance

Transaction or payment data (e.g., rent billing records) required by law will be retained for 180 days before being permanently deleted.

If you have questions about the deletion process, please contact us at support@risenholdings.com.tw.
